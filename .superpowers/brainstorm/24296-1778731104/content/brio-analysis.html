What I found from exploring briocafekw.com

Pages & Structure

Homepage

Hero banner, cafe story, featured items, location info

2

Explore Menu (Shop)

Category grid: Hot Beverages, Ice Beverages, Pastries, Add Ons, Beans, Matcha, etc.

3

Category Page

Sub-category cards + product grid with images, names, prices

4

Product Detail

Image, description, modifiers (size, milk, addons), Add to Cart

5

Cart

Line items, qty adjust, remove, subtotal, checkout button

6

Checkout

Customer form, order summary, payment

7

Contact Us

Contact form, location map, cafe info

Design Language

  • Clean white background with green brand accent
  • Minimal navigation: Home | Explore Menu | Contact Us
  • Cart icon with item count + total (د.ك currency)
  • Category cards with photos and product counts
  • Simple product cards with image, name, price

Key Features Observed

  • Product modifiers (e.g., milk type, size, addons)
  • Category hierarchy (Add Ons as cross-category)
  • WooCommerce-style cart/checkout
  • QR/table ordering not visible (pure e-commerce flow)
